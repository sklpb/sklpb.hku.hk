Yeast Two-hybrid (Y2H) Screening

Yeast two-hybrid (Y2H) screening is a mature technique to discover novel protein interactions and maps the precise binding regions between two proteins. Our service includes:

  • Cloning: Subclone gene of interest into Y2H vector and transform into yeast.
  • Testing for “bait” auto-activation.
  • Screening:
    • - Perform large scale (pre-made or custom-made) library screen.
    - Phenotypic analysis (up to 4 reporter genes are used).
    - Recover plasmids from yeast and transform into E.coli.
    - Determine identity of “prey” by sequencing.
  • Validation: Retransform yeast with recovered plasmids to verify interactions.
